# Cost Optimization Project - Adventure Works

## Objective

Deliver a comprehensive Azure cost optimization engagement for Adventure Works, identifying savings opportunities, providing actionable recommendations, and supporting implementation of cost reduction measures.

## Key Goals

1. **Assessment**: Analyze current Azure spending patterns and resource utilization for Adventure Works
2. **Recommendations**: Identify and prioritize cost optimization opportunities
3. **Implementation Support**: Guide Adventure Works through implementing approved recommendations
4. **Documentation**: Provide detailed SOW, kickoff materials, and summary presentation

## Success Criteria

- Identify minimum 15% potential cost savings
- Deliver actionable recommendations within 2 weeks
- Support Adventure Works in implementing quick wins
- Provide executive summary for Adventure Works leadership
