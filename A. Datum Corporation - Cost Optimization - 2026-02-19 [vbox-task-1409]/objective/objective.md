# Cost Optimization Project - A. Datum Corporation

## Objective

Deliver a comprehensive Azure cost optimization engagement for A. Datum Corporation, identifying savings opportunities, providing actionable recommendations, and supporting implementation of cost reduction measures.

## Key Goals

1. **Assessment**: Analyze current Azure spending patterns and resource utilization for A. Datum Corporation
2. **Recommendations**: Identify and prioritize cost optimization opportunities
3. **Implementation Support**: Guide A. Datum Corporation through implementing approved recommendations
4. **Documentation**: Provide detailed SOW, kickoff materials, and summary presentation

## Success Criteria

- Identify minimum 15% potential cost savings
- Deliver actionable recommendations within 2 weeks
- Support A. Datum Corporation in implementing quick wins
- Provide executive summary for A. Datum Corporation leadership
